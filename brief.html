The Gap

Coaching shouldn't depend on memory

Managers review PMM work constantly. But without a system, feedback is inconsistent, growth is invisible, and appraisal season turns into guesswork.

Without Master Reviewer

  • Feedback varies by manager mood and workload
  • No record of what was said or whether it was applied
  • "Did they actually improve?" is answered with gut feel
  • Same feedback given repeatedly, nothing changes
  • Promotion cases rely on anecdotes, not evidence

With Master Reviewer

  • Every document scored with the same 29-skill rubric
  • Every round of feedback tracked: was it applied?
  • Growth is quantified — from first draft to final, and across projects
  • Persistent gaps are flagged so coaching can change approach
  • Promotion reports backed by data: "improved 4.2 points across 6 projects"
How It Works

From document to coaching insight in minutes

Submit two versions of any PMM document. Master Reviewer does the rest.

Submit Drafts

2

4

Track Growth

Profile updated with scores, patterns, and trends that build over time

Built for Humans

Every review gives exactly 3 priorities

29 skills are scored for tracking, but people can only absorb 2-3 improvements at a time. Every review selects the 3 highest-impact actions and parks the rest.
