# DataSync Pro — Product Messaging Document

## Overview

DataSync Pro is a unified data integration platform built for data teams who are tired of brittle pipelines and manual data wrangling. It connects your entire data stack — from SaaS apps to warehouses to operational databases — with real-time sync that just works.

## The Problem We Solve

Data teams spend 40% of their time on pipeline maintenance instead of analysis (Gartner, 2025). Every broken pipeline means delayed reports, stale dashboards, and decisions made on yesterday's data. The cost isn't just engineering hours — it's the business decisions that don't get made.

## Who This Is For

**Primary persona: Data Engineering Lead (Mid-Market, 500-5000 employees)**

They manage a growing data stack with 15-30 connected systems. They've outgrown Stitch or manual scripts but don't have the budget or team size for a full Informatica deployment. They need:
- Reliability without babysitting — they're a team of 3-5, not 30
- Self-serve access for analysts — stop being a bottleneck
- Visibility into pipeline health without building custom monitoring

**Secondary persona: VP of Data / Head of Analytics**
Cares about time-to-insight, total cost of ownership, and vendor consolidation.

## Value Proposition

**For data teams drowning in pipeline maintenance, DataSync Pro is the integration platform that eliminates sync failures before they happen** — so your team spends time on analysis, not firefighting.

Unlike Fivetran (which charges per row and locks you into their connector ecosystem) or self-managed Airbyte (which trades licensing costs for DevOps overhead), DataSync Pro gives you:

- **Predictable pricing** at flat-rate per connector, not per row
- **Zero-maintenance reliability** with automatic schema drift detection and self-healing pipelines
- **Self-serve for analysts** via a visual pipeline builder that doesn't require SQL or engineering tickets

## Proof Points

- Ramp reduced pipeline incidents by 73% in the first 90 days after switching from custom scripts
- Vanta's data team went from 8-hour SLA on new data requests to under 30 minutes using self-serve connectors
- 99.97% uptime across all managed pipelines in 2025 (published status page)
- 200+ pre-built connectors, with new ones shipping weekly based on customer votes

## Messaging Pillars

### 1. "Pipelines that fix themselves"
Your data infrastructure should work for you, not the other way around. Automatic schema drift detection and self-healing pipelines mean your team gets paged less and ships more.

No per-row surprises. No "call us for enterprise pricing." Flat-rate per connector means you know exactly what you'll pay before you connect.
